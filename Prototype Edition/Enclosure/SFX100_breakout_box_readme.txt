Use this enclosure if your PCB has v1.32 printed on it.

Recommended print settings for enclosure

0.2mm layer height
20% infill
4 perimeters

Mounting hardware for PCB:
4x M3x16
2x M3x4

Cap head screws are recommended, as some of the screw holes are near solder points. Screws with larger heads may damage the solder point.
